# How Do Geopolitical Tensions Shape Cross-Border Capital Investment?

This repository contains all the material related to the master's thesis by **Chiara Bargellesi** at the **London School of Economics (LSE)**. The thesis investigates how geopolitical tensions influence cross-border capital investment.

## Repository Contents
 
- **Thesis Document**: The full thesis in PDF format.  

## About the Author

Chiara Bargellesi completed this work as part of her Master's program at the **London School of Economics**, combining empirical analysis and economic theory to understand international investment patterns under geopolitical stress.
